## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Data Collected from LinkedIn

When you connect your LinkedIn account to Trend Hunting AI, we collect the following types of data through LinkedIn APIs:

**Profile Data:**
- Full name
- Profile photo
- Email address
- Headline and summary
- Current position and company
- Location
- Professional experience and education

**Posts and Articles:**
- Posts you have published
- Articles you have written
- Engagement metrics (impressions, likes, comments, shares)
- Post timestamps and content

**Organization Data (if you are an admin):**
- Company Page information
- Follower statistics
- Page post performance
- Demographic data of followers

**Activity Data:**
- Reactions to posts by others
- Comments made on posts
- Content you have shared

### 2.2 Data We Generate

Based on the data collected, we generate:
- Analytics and insights about your LinkedIn performance
- Trend reports and benchmarks
- Engagement predictions and recommendations

### 2.3 Technical Data

We automatically collect:
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- Usage patterns and interactions with our platform
- Log files and error reports

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Storage Location

Your data is stored on secure servers provided by **Hetzner** (Germany), which complies with GDPR standards.

### 5.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

**Encryption:**
- Data in transit: TLS 1.3
- Data at rest: AES-256 encryption

**Access Control:**
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) for admin access
- Regular security audits and penetration testing

**Monitoring:**
- 24/7 system monitoring
- Automated threat detection
- Incident response procedures

### 5.3 Data Retention

We retain your data according to the following principles:

**Active Users:**
- Data is retained as long as your account is active
- You can request deletion at any time

**Inactive Users:**
- If you do not use Trend Hunting AI for 12 consecutive months, we will send a notification
- After 18 months of inactivity, your data will be automatically deleted

**Legal Obligations:**
- Some data may be retained longer to comply with legal requirements:
  - **Tax records:** 5 years (Brazilian Tax Code)
  - **Consumer protection:** 5 years (Brazilian Consumer Defense Code)
  - **Audit logs:** 6 months (Marco Civil da Internet - Brazil)

**After Account Deletion:**
- Personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days
- Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained for statistical purposes

## 6. Your Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and LGPD, you have the following rights:

### 6.1 Right to Access
- Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
- Receive data in a structured, machine-readable format (data portability)

### 6.2 Right to Rectification
- Correct inaccurate or incomplete data

### 6.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
- Request deletion of your personal data
- We will comply within 30 days unless legal obligations require retention

### 6.4 Right to Restrict Processing
- Limit how we use your data in certain circumstances

### 6.5 Right to Object
- Object to processing based on legitimate interest
- Opt-out of marketing communications

### 6.6 Right to Withdraw Consent
- Revoke your consent at any time
- Disconnect your LinkedIn account from Trend Hunting AI

### 6.7 Right to Data Portability
- Export your data to another service

### 6.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint
- File a complaint with supervisory authorities:
  - **Brazil:** ANPD (Autoridade Nacional de Proteção de Dados)
  - **EU:** Your local Data Protection Authority
  - **California:** California Attorney General
